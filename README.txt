Veil Discord Control Center

Setup:
1. Extract ZIP.
2. Open PowerShell in this folder.
3. Run: py -m pip install -r requirements.txt
4. Run: copy .env.example .env
5. Open .env and paste your real bot token.
6. Run: py run.py
7. Open http://127.0.0.1:8787

Discord commands:
!setup
!memoryinfo
!setstatus your status
!sendembed products shop_panel
!postproducts products
!ticketpanel create-ticket
!vouchpanel vouches

Do not share .env or data/app.db.
